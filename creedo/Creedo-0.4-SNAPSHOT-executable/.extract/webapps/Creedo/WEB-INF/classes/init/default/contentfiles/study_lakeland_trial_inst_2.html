Ready for the real tasks? Before that, take some time to go over the step-by-step instructions blow of how to operating our mining tool.

The dashboard of the mining tool consists of four main components:

  • Data Area (fig 1.a) gives you the overview of the data. Within this area, each data column corresponds to a socio-economic attribute. By pointing the cursor at the header of each data column (fig 1.b), you will see the elementary statistics of that column.

  • Mine Button (fig 1.c) allows you to request new patterns by a simple click.

  • Candidate Area (fig 1.d) will display the proposed patterns.

  • Result Area (fig 1.e) allows you to assemble your results.

Typically, a discovery process consists of following steps:

  1. Study the statistics of each column, and get some elementary knowledge of your country's socio-economic status.

  2. Once you are done, click the mine button and the proposed patterns will show up in the candidate area. This might take some time, thank you for your patience.

  3. Investigate the candidate patterns and save a pattern that you found interesting by dragging it (fig 2.a) to the result area (fig 2.b).

  4. You may delete a non-interesting patterns by clicking the right upper cross icon (fig 2.c).

  5. Repeat step II. and IV., refine your pattern selection until you think you have discovered the most interesting three patterns to describe the socio-economic status of Lakeland.

  6. When you are satisfied with the results, submit the discovered result by click on the "Submit" button (fig 2.d).

Please click on "Next" to start.
