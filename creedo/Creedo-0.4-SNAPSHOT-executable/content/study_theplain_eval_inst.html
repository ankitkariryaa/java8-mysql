As the governor of The Plain, you are requested to evaluate the work of the new employees in the Data Science Department. Please read the following instructions to get familiar with the rating system:

  1. On the main dashboard of our mining tool, you can browse the data in area (fig. a). Each data column corresponds to a socio-economic attribute (fig. b). By pointing the cursor at the header of each data column, you will see the elementary statistics of that column. Please carefully study the statistics of each column.

  2. You may investigate the results discovered by some employees in area (fig. c). Each result consists of three patterns.

  3. To rate a pattern, fist click on it to select (the pattern will be highlighted), then click on the corresponding value of the right-hand-side rating criteria (fig. d):How much new knowledge about the The Plain do you gain from the current result, in addition to the elementary statistics of the attributes?

  4. Once you have rated all results, click "Submit" to save (fig. e).

Pleas click on "Next" to start.
